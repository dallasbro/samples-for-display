Hello and welcome to the Broyles' Camping Year for 2010.

I, Craig Broyles, recently took an HTML course in college and had to develop a website for a final project. So I thought I would take the time and build it about something I was interested in. Camping with friends and family.

Throughout the course I learned many things like developing style sheets both internal and external, as well as, building web tables, web forms and inline frames.

The Home page (current page) is an introduction to the site. At the top you will see links to other pages within the site. The Camp Dates page inludes a web table of a calendar which has the dates of all of our planned camping trips for the 2010 season. The Links page provides links to the camping locations and places of interest in the area. The Rally with Us page displays a web form that lets us know you are planning to meet us at a specific location so we can make proper campsite arrangements. I hope you enjoy the website and I hope to rally with you this camping season.
